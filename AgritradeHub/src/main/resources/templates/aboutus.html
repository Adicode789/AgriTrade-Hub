About the Client

Based in Barabanki, Uttar Pradesh, Hukmawati Cold Storage is a leading agri-logistics provider offering state-of-the-art cold storage facilities. By preserving produce and reducing post-harvest losses, they help farmers maximize their profits.

Their focus on modernizing agri-logistics makes them an ideal partner for AgriTrade Hub.
